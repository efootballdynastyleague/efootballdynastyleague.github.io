League Rules

Official rules that guide all managers, fixtures, player conduct, transfers, complaints, and tournament participation.

Match Rules

  • Managers must play fixtures within the scheduled period.
  • Results must be submitted with clear proof when required.
  • Disconnections should be reported immediately.

Manager Conduct

  • Respect all managers and admins.
  • No abusive language, cheating, or fake results.
  • Complaints must be submitted through the official complaint channel.

Admin Decisions

  • Admins review complaints and evidence.
  • Admins may award wins, order replays, or issue penalties.
  • Final decisions are recorded for transparency.
